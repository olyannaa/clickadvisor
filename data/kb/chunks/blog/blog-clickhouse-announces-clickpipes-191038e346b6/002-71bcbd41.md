The company recently announced the debut of proprietary [SharedMergeTree](/blog/clickhouse-cloud-boosts-performance-with-sharedmergetree-and-lightweight-updates) table engine, boosting performance of ClickHouse Cloud, and its fully managed cloud offering is now available on Google Cloud Platform as well as Amazon Web Services.

**About ClickHouse**

ClickHouse is the world's fastest and most resource\-efficient online analytical column\-oriented database management system. Now offered as a secure and scalable serverless offering in the cloud, ClickHouse Cloud allows anyone to effortlessly take advantage of efficient real\-time analytical processing.
