One explain: "At Capital One, we want to give teams the data they need, when they need it, to make informed FinOps decisions. With ClickHouse, we can: spot trends early … foster shared accountability … and plan smarter."

Across the financial services ecosystem, organizations are leveraging ClickHouse for mission\-critical workloads. Enterprises like [Deutsche Bank](https://clickhouse.com/videos/big-sensitive-data-warehouse-deutsche-bank-clickhouse) rely on ClickHouse for enterprise\-scale analytics of financial services data, while [RBC](https://rbcborealis.com/news/clickhouse-adoption-at-rbc-borealis/) leverages ClickHouse to power AI and machine learning workloads. Fintech innovators like [Longbridge](https://clickhouse.com/blog/longbridge-technology-simplifies-their-architecture-and-achieves-10x-performance-boost-with-clickhouse) use ClickHouse to power real\-time market data analytics for traders and quant teams. In the payments space, [Juspay](https://clickhouse.com/blog/juspay-analyzes-payment-transactions-in-real-time-with-clickhouse) and [ProcessOut](https://clickhouse.com/blog/processout-switched-from-elasticsearch-to-clickhouse-cloud) leverage ClickHouse to analyze billions of transactions with high concurrency, enabling better fraud detection and improved operational efficiency.

The cryptocurrency and Web3 sector have also embraced ClickHouse. [Merkle Science](https://clickhouse.com/blog/merkle-science-fights-crypto-crime-at-scale), [Coinhall](https://clickhouse.com/blog/trade-secrets-how-coinhall-uses-clickhouse-to-power-its-blockchain-data-platform), and [Lens](https://clickhouse.com/blog/rockset-migration-to-clickhouse-lens) use ClickHouse to monitor trading activity, detect anomalies, and deliver real\-time insights. Meanwhile, [Seon](https://clickhouse.com/blog/seon-clickhouse-aws) depends on ClickHouse for their fraud prevention and anti\-money laundering platform.

Consumer fintech companies such as [Superwall](https://clickhouse.com/blog/superwall-warpstream-clickhouse-cloud-monetization) and [QuickCheck](https://clickhouse.com/blog/how-quickcheck-uses-clickhouse-to-bring-banking-to-the-unbanked) use ClickHouse to track customer transactions, interactions, and behavioral patterns, enabling personalized offerings and deeper customer insights at scale.
