## Why this is faster in practice [\#](/blog/streaming-secondary-indices#why-this-is-faster-in-practice)

As a reminder, the observed speedup comes from two (в‘  and в‘Ў) mechanisms:

- **Without streaming indices**: Before query processing even begins, ClickHouse must fully scan and process the index to identify all matching granules.
- **With streaming indices**:  

в‘  ClickHouse concurrently scans the index and processes matching granules in the query engine.  

в‘Ў And as soon as the first matching row (LIMIT 1\) is found, it immediately stops scanning further index entries and granules, eliminating wasted work.
