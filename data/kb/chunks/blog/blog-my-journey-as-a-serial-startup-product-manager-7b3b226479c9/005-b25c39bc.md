But ultimately, I believe that a product manager is a leader that wakes up every morning, thinks about how to make their product successful in the market, and does what it takes to make it happen. The best and most succinct way I have seen this philosophy described is in Ben Horowitz [Good PM / Bad PM blog](https://a16z.com/2012/06/15/good-product-managerbad-product-manager/). He says it is dated, but I think it stands the test of time.
