## Featured community member [\#](/blog/202501-newsletter#featured-community-member)

This month's featured community member is [Jason Anderson](https://www.linkedin.com/in/andersonljason/), Head of Data at Skool, a community platform.

![featured-member-202501.png](/uploads/featured_member_202501_664885507d.png)

Jason Anderson is an experienced data and technology professional with a background in leading teams and developing data\-driven solutions. He previously served as Head of Data at Mythical Games and Partner at Comp Three, focusing on machine learning, analytics, and cloud architecture. His career includes roles at IBM and PolySat, where he contributed to cloud services and satellite software development.

Jason recently [presented his work at Skool at the ClickHouse Los Angeles meetup](https://clickhouse.com/videos/skools-journey-with-clickhouse). Jason explained how they moved from Postgres to ClickHouse to process 100M\+ rows daily while delivering lightning\-fast queries. There is also [a blog post that explains Skool’s use of ClickHouse in more detail](https://clickhouse.com/blog/how-skool-uses-clickhouse-for-observability-behavioral-analytics).
