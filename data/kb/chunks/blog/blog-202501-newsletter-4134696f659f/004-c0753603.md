- [Hellmar Becker](https://www.linkedin.com/in/hellmarbecker/) recently joined ClickHouse and has been taking it through its paces. In his first blog post, he explores [array processing functions](https://blog.hellmar-becker.de/2025/01/01/new-years-greetings-from-the-data-cookbook-elf/), and in the second, we learn how to do [linear algebra in ClickHouse](https://blog.hellmar-becker.de/2025/01/05/clickhouse-data-cookbook-linear-algebra-in-sql/).
- [Hardik Singh Behl](https://www.linkedin.com/in/hardiksinghbehl/) explores [how to integrate ClickHouse into a Spring Boot application](https://www.baeldung.com/spring-boot-olap-clickhouse-database). He first configures the application and establishes a database connection before performing a few CRUD operations.
- Andrei Tserakhau shows [how to transfer data from MySQL to ClickHouse](https://medium.com/@laskoviymishka/cdc-from-mysql-to-clickhouse-c791fe414fe1) using Transfer, an open\-source cloud\-native ingestion engine.
- [Shivji kumar Jha](https://www.linkedin.com/in/shivjijha/) explores [how Postgres and ClickHouse can work together as a unified data management solution](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/unified-data-platforms-ft-postgres-clickhouse-shivji-kumar-jha-jylqc/), balancing transactional reliability with high\-speed analytics.

## Video corner [\#](/blog/202501-newsletter#video-corner)

- We had two ClickHouse speakers at the [All Things Open 2024 conference](https://2024.allthingsopen.org/speakers). Tanya Bragin explored [how open source technologies and datalake standards are transforming the modern data stack](https://clickhouse.com/videos/all-things-open-open-source-cloud-datawarehouse) by offering alternatives to monolithic cloud data warehouses.
- Zoe Steinkamp explained [how columnar databases are revolutionizing data warehousing](https://clickhouse.com/videos/all-things-open-columnar-storage) and analytics by offering superior performance over traditional row\-based systems. Zoe also demonstrated how we can build efficient analytics applications with tools like Apache Arrow, Parquet, and Pandas while reducing costs and improving query performance./li\>
- Mark explained the [various deployment modes of ClickHouse](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EOXEW_-r10A&t=5s), including ClickHouse Server, clickhouse\-local, and chDB.
- Avi Press explains how Scarf has [built a ClickHouse\-backed data pipeline that handles \~25GB of data and 50 million events every day](https://clickhouse.com/videos/open-source-scarf).
