ClickHouse to prepare data for dense model generation for one of their clients. It took a pretty short time on a single server. Fun quote from conclusions: “If I wasn't already married, I'd marry it! \[ClickHouse]” ([slides](https://presentations.clickhouse.com/meetup21/predictive_models.pdf)): ![2019-meetup-madrid-april-5.jpeg](/uploads/2019_meetup_madrid_april_5_d7db725501.jpeg)

Closing talk of the meetup was by Murat Kabilov fro Adjust Gmbh, he was demonstrating his opensource project [pg2ch](https://github.com/mkabilov/pg2ch) that allows to sync data from PostgreSQL to ClickHouse in real\-time ([slides](https://presentations.clickhouse.com/meetup21/postgres_to_clickhouse.pdf)).
