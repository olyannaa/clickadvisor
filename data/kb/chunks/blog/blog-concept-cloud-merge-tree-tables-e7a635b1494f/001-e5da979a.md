# Concept: "Cloud" MergeTree Tables

![ClickHouse Team](/_next/image?url=%2Fuploads%2Flogo_square_120_2cca16e3e3.png&w=96&q=75)[ClickHouse Editor](/authors/clickhouse-editor)Nov 23, 2018 · 20 minutes readThe main property of the MergeTree cloud tables is the absence of manual control over the sharding scheme of data on a cluster. The data in the cloud tables are distributed around the cluster on its own, while at the same time providing the locality property for a certain key.
