Database upgrades follow a [make\-before\-break strategy](https://clickhouse.com/blog/make-before-break-faster-scaling-mechanics-for-clickhouse-cloud): updated replicas are added before old ones are removed, minimizing disruption. You can select Fast, Regular, or Slow release channels, with upgrades aligned to your configured maintenance window. ### Get started today

BYOC on Google Cloud is available now. Contact our support team or reach out to your account team for a guided onboarding session. The BYOC documentation covers the full architecture, security model, and configuration options in detail.
