Ubicloud is building the open\-source AWS, delivering core cloud services across bare metal and public clouds. Founded by the team that built distributed PostgreSQL with Citus Data (acquired by Microsoft), Ubicloud’s flagship database offering, Ubicloud PostgreSQL provides an enterprise\-grade managed Postgres experience with industry\-leading price\-performance. Ubicloud services across AI, Compute, PostgreSQL, and Kubernetes power more than 1 million VMs every week, cutting cloud costs by up to 70%. Ubicloud is backed by Y Combinator and other prominent Silicon Valley investors.
