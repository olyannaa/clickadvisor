# Learning ClickHouse \- A Story about Community

![](/_next/image?url=%2Fuploads%2FIMG_5232_1a1f71d202.jpg&w=96&q=75)Tim DavisJul 7, 2023 · 6 minutes readWell, hello there! Let me introduce myself. I am [Tim](https://twitter.com/vtimd), the newest Developer Advocate here at ClickHouse! I’ve been here for about 2 months now, but you may not have even heard much from me yet! I’ve been around, but I’ve been busy learning. ClickHouse provides a really supportive environment that allows new team members to get up to speed and set themselves up for success. It’s actually quite refreshing as opposed to having to just hit the ground running and produce on day 1, while you try and catch up at the same time. It’s a really great work environment, and I highly recommend it. Speaking of, if this all sounds like your cup of tea, we have positions open! Check them out [here](/company/careers)!

Ok, back to the point! This blog isn’t just to tell you about how great it is to work at ClickHouse. Though it is. I wanted to take a moment to talk about the multitude of resources I have been using to get up to speed with ClickHouse. I will be the first to admit that I was, and still am, absolutely not a ClickHouse expert. The good news with that, is that we get to learn together. And I get to help show you helpful hints, tips, and maybe some pitfalls along the way. So, how many different places are there to learn about ClickHouse? Let’s find out!
