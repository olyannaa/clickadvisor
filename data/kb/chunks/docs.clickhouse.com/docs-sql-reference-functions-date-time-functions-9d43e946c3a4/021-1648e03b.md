## makeDate[‚Äã](#makeDate "Direct link to makeDate")

Introduced in: v22\.6\.0

Creates a `Date` from either:

- a year, month and day
- a year and day of year

**Syntax**

```
makeDate(year, month, day)
makeDate(year, day_of_year)

```

**Arguments**

- `year` ‚Äî Year number. [`(U)Int*`](/docs/sql-reference/data-types/int-uint) or [`Float*`](/docs/sql-reference/data-types/float) or [`Decimal`](/docs/sql-reference/data-types/decimal)
- `month` ‚Äî Month number (1\-12\). [`(U)Int*`](/docs/sql-reference/data-types/int-uint) or [`Float*`](/docs/sql-reference/data-types/float) or [`Decimal`](/docs/sql-reference/data-types/decimal)
- `day` ‚Äî Day of the month (1\-31\). [`(U)Int*`](/docs/sql-reference/data-types/int-uint) or [`Float*`](/docs/sql-reference/data-types/float) or [`Decimal`](/docs/sql-reference/data-types/decimal)
- `day_of_year` ‚Äî Day of the year (1\-365\). [`(U)Int*`](/docs/sql-reference/data-types/int-uint) or [`Float*`](/docs/sql-reference/data-types/float) or [`Decimal`](/docs/sql-reference/data-types/decimal)

**Returned value**

Returns a `Date` value constructed from the provided arguments [`Date`](/docs/sql-reference/data-types/date)

**Examples**

**Date from a year, month, day**

```
SELECT makeDate(2023, 2, 28) AS date;

```
